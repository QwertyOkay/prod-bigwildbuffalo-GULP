Return Policy

At PlayAndina, we want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase of gaming controllers, gaming mice, pads, and keyboards. If, for any reason, you are not completely happy with your product, we offer a hassle-free return policy. Please review the following guidelines:

1. Eligibility: To be eligible for a return, the item must be in its original condition, unused, and in the same packaging as when you received it. The return must be initiated within 14 days from the date of purchase.

2. Return Process: To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team. They will guide you through the process and provide you with the necessary instructions. You may be asked to provide proof of purchase and any relevant details about the item.

3. Return Shipping: The cost of return shipping will be your responsibility. We recommend using a reliable shipping method with tracking and insurance to ensure the safe return of the item. Please note that we are not liable for any damages or losses that occur during the return shipping process.

4. Refund or Exchange: Once we receive the returned item and verify its condition, we will process your refund or exchange. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. If you opt for an exchange, we will ship the new item to you promptly.

5. Non-Returnable Items: Please note that certain items, such as personalized or customized products, are non-returnable unless there is a manufacturing defect or error on our part.

6. Restocking Fee: In some cases, a restocking fee may apply for returns of certain items. This fee covers the cost of inspecting, testing, and restocking the product to ensure it is in resalable condition.

7. Damaged or Defective Items: If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact us immediately. We will work with you to resolve the issue promptly, either by providing a replacement or issuing a refund.
